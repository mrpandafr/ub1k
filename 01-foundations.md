# 1. Foundations (1998–2018)

A math thesis on vector spaces, geometry, distances between concepts. Twenty years later, those same math became vector memory.

**Key references:**
- Distributional hypothesis — Harris (1954)
- LSA — Landauer & Dumais (1997)
- Word2Vec — Mikolov et al. (2013)

The core insight: words in similar contexts have similar meanings. This is the foundation of all embedding models.
